  • meanings of \(F_2^{charm}\) (the weight coming from the suitable line of the CKM matrix) (and analogously for bottom and top)

\[\begin{split}V_{CKM} = \begin{pmatrix} {\color{red}V_{ud}} & {\color{red}V_{us}} & {\color{green}V_{ub}}\\ {\color{blue}V_{cd}} & {\color{blue}V_{cs}} & {\color{green}V_{cb}}\\ {\color{purple}V_{td}} & {\color{purple}V_{ts}} & {\color{purple}V_{tb}} \end{pmatrix}\end{split}\]

actual definition of coefficient functions weights in \(F_2\) (with an initial neutrino, \(\nu\)):

\[\begin{split}\begin{array}{rcl} F_2^{\nu,p} &=& 2x\Big\{C_{2,q}\otimes \Big[\left(|{\color{red}V_{ud}}|^2+|{\color{blue}V_{cd}}|^2+|{\color{purple}V_{td}}|^2\right)d\\ &+& \left(|{\color{red}V_{ud}}|^2+|{\color{red}V_{us}}|^2+|{\color{green}V_{ub}}|^2\right)\overline{u}\\ &+& \left(|{\color{red}V_{us}}|^2+|{\color{blue}V_{cs}}|^2+|{\color{purple}V_{ts}}|^2\right)s\\ &+& \left(|{\color{blue}V_{cd}}|^2+|{\color{blue}V_{cs}}|^2+|{\color{green}V_{cb}}|^2\right)\overline{c}\\ &+& \left(|{\color{green}V_{ub}}|^2+|{\color{green}V_{cb}}|^2+|{\color{purple}V_{tb}}|^2\right)b\\ &+& \left(|{\color{purple}V_{td}}|^2+|{\color{purple}V_{ts}}|^2+|{\color{purple}V_{tb}}|^2\right)\overline{t}\Big]\\ &+& c^{CC}_g(N_f)C_{2,q}\otimes g\Big\} \end{array}\end{split}\]

and in \(F_2^{charm}\):

\[\begin{split}{\color{blue} F_{2,c}^{\color{black} \nu,p}} &=& 2x\Big\{C_{2,q}\otimes\Big[|{\color{blue}V_{cd}}|^2(d+\overline{c}) + |{\color{blue}V_{cs}}|^2 (s+\overline{c})\Big]\\ &+& 2\left(|{\color{blue}V_{cd}}|^2+|{\color{blue}V_{cs}}|^2\right)C_{2,g}\otimes g\Big\}\\\end{split}\]

Asymptotics for FONLL¶

For FONLL we need the massless limit of the coefficient functions in [GKR96]. We obtain \(\lambda\to 1, \xi \to x\) and

  • light quark channel:

\[\begin{split}H_i^{q,asy} &= P_{qq}(z) \ln (Q^2/\mu_F^2) + h_i^{q,asy}(z)\\ h_i^{q,asy} &= C_F \left[ -\left(\frac 9 2 + \frac{\pi^2}{3} \right)\delta(1-z) - \frac{1+z^2}{1-z} \ln(z) + (1+z^2)\left( \frac{\ln(1-z)}{1-z}\right)_+ \right.\\ &\hspace{40pt} + \left. B^{(i)}\left(\frac 1 {1-z}\right)_+ \right]\end{split}\]

with \(K_A \to 0\) and the coefficients \(B^{(i)} = B_{1,i} + B_{2,i} + B_{3,i}\) given by

\(B^{(1)}\)

\(\frac 3 2 - 3 z\)

\(B^{(2)}\)

\(\frac 3 2 - z - 2z^2\)

\(B^{(3)}\)

\(\frac 1 2 - z - z^2\)

For \(F_L = F_2 - 2xF_1\) we obtain:

\[\begin{split}H_L^{q,asy} &= H_2^{q,asy} - H_1^{q,asy} \\ &= C_F \left(B^{(2)} - B^{(1)}\right)\left(\frac 1 {1-z}\right)_+\\ &= C_F \cdot 2z\end{split}\]

  • gluon channel:

\[\begin{split}H_{i=1,2/3}^{g,asy} &= P_{qg}(z)\left(\pm\left(\ln((1-z)/z) + \ln(Q^2/m^2)\right) + \ln (Q^2/\mu_F^2)\right) + h_i^{g,asy}(z)\\ h_i^{g,asy} &= P_{qg}(z) \ln((1-z)/z) + C_{1,i}^{asy} z(1-z) + C_{2,i}^{asy}\end{split}\]

with the coefficients \(C_{i,j}^{asy}\) given by

For \(F_L = F_2 - 2xF_1\) we obtain:

\[\begin{split}H_L^{g,asy} &= H_2^{g,asy} - H_1^{g,asy} \\ &= \left(C_{1,2}^{asy} - C_{1,1}^{asy}\right) z(1-z) + \left(C_{2,2}^{asy} - C_{2,1}^{asy}\right)\\ &= 4z(1-z)\end{split}\]
